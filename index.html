Why are we here ?


More than 200 Million Indians eat on 10 Million + street food carts everyday. Yet, most of the street food vendors are living below the poverty line.


One of the few reasons are:


  • Unlicensed carts
  • Unhygenic food
  • Unprofitable Business

We are here with a solution that will redefine the street food experience; The Next Generation Street Food Carts!



Come join us in the journey of transforming your Street Food experience in India!
