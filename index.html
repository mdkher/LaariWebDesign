Why are we here ?


More than 200 Million people eat on 10 Million + street food carts daily in India.


Still, most of the street food vendors are living below pioverty line.

The biggest reasons behind this are :


  • Unlicensed carts
  • Unhygenic food
  • Highly unprofitable business

We are solving this problem by redefining street food experience and manufacturing the next generation street food carts !



Come join us in out journey of transformation of Middle India !
